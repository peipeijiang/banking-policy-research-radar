<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

用于估计风险厌恶的标准随机选择模型可能导致风险厌恶反转，即一个更厌恶风险的个体比一个较不厌恶风险的个体更频繁地选择风险更高的彩票。我们研究了反转是由偏好设定还是噪声设定所导致的情况。我们指出，在给定的噪声框架下，如果对于每个个体的每一种噪声设定都会出现反转，那么两种效用函数就蕴含了反转。对于弱效用函数（weak utility）——这一灵活类别包括逻辑模型（logit）和概率模型（probit），并允许依赖于菜单的噪声——两种效用函数蕴含反转当且仅当其曲率比（curvature ratio）是无界的。这一条件适用于常绝对风险厌恶（CARA）、常相对风险厌恶（CRRA）及其推广形式，对于这些模型，反转会在经验相关的系数和彩票中出现，从而引发对由此产生的估计结果及样本外预测的担忧。最后，我们证明等谨慎的HARA（equicautious HARA）、指数和（sum-ex）以及幂和（sum-power）效用函数并不蕴含反转，而且这些函数族在多项选择（multinomial choice）中仍然保持良好的性质。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

Standard stochastic choice models used to estimate risk aversion can lead to risk-aversion reversals, where a more risk-averse individual chooses a riskier lottery more frequently than a less risk-averse individual. We study when reversals are implied by the preference specification rather than the noise specification. We say that two utilities imply reversals in a given noise framework if reversals arise for every specification of noise for each individual. For weak utility, a flexible class that includes logit and probit and allows for menu-dependent noise, two utilities imply reversals if and only if their curvature ratio is unbounded. This condition holds for CARA, CRRA, and their generalizations, for which reversals arise for empirically relevant coefficients and lotteries, raising concerns about resulting estimates and out-of-sample predictions. Finally, we show that equicautious HARA, sum-ex, and sum-power utilities do not imply reversals and that, moreover, these families remain well-behaved for multinomial choice.
