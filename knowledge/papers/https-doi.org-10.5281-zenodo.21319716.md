随机品牌选择传统——马尔可夫切换模型与NBD-Dirichlet模型——通过将品牌选择倾向视为外生给定因素而取得了描述性成功：这些模型定位了倾向所隐含的均衡，但对倾向的成因保持沉默。本文提供并检验了缺失的生成层。在队列层面，光谱匹配（spectral match）——即观察者队列的八维感知轮廓与品牌轮廓之间的相似性统计量——被提出作为该队列在其品类考虑集内所陈述选择倾向的单调预测因子。一个倾向向量以封闭形式诱导出零阶Dirichlet切换矩阵。因此，感知层设定了该传统所出发的矩阵：双重困境对角线（double-jeopardy diagonal）被继承，中间倾向带成为模型推导的结果，而感知上同分异构（perceptually metameric）的品牌对必须获得统计上不可区分的倾向向量——这是任何基于效用或基于一致性（congruity）的论述都无法做出的预测。在一项预先注册的、在严格工具分离条件下进行、包含四个淘汰条件（kill conditions）的LLM队列实验中，该关联在实验层面成立（τ_b = .323, p < .001；一个随机轮廓的安慰剂破坏了这一关联），在快餐咖啡品类中得到复制，在运动鞋品类中失败，且未显示共同方法膨胀（common-method inflation，Δτ = -.002）；同分异构品牌对违反了倾向相等性，恰好将感知层的充分性限定在真实品牌轮廓趋于收敛之处。包含zharnikov-2026bf-perception-sets-matrix.yaml（论文规范v0.1.0）——一份机器可读的论文主张、假设与依赖关系规范。论文的完整机器优先包（SPINE主张/依赖关系图与ONTOLOGY术语模块）存放于公共仓库中；标准参见https://github.com/spectralbranding/paper-spec。本PDF是在研究即仓库（research-as-repository）模型下，从该机器优先源程序化生成的。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

The stochastic brand-choice tradition — Markov switching models and the NBD-Dirichlet — earns its descriptive success by taking brand-choice propensities as exogenous givens: the models locate the equilibrium the propensities imply and are silent on what sets them. This paper supplies and tests the missing generative layer. At cohort level, spectral match — a similarity statistic between an observer cohort's eight-dimensional perception profile and a brand's profile — is proposed as a monotone predictor of the cohort's stated choice propensity within its category consideration set. A propensity vector induces the zero-order Dirichlet switching matrix in closed form. The perceptual layer therefore sets the matrix the tradition starts from: the double-jeopardy diagonal is inherited, the intermediate-propensity band becomes model-derived, and perceptually metameric brand pairs must receive statistically indistinguishable propensity vectors — a prediction no utility-based or congruity-based account makes. In a pre-registered LLM-cohort campaign under strict instrument separation with four kill conditions, the link held experimentally (τ_b= .323, p < .001; a random-profile placebo destroys it), replicated in quick-service coffee, failed in athletic footwear, and showed no common-method inflation (Δτ= -.002); metameric brand pairs violated propensity equality, bounding the perceptual layer's sufficiency exactly where real brand profiles converge.
