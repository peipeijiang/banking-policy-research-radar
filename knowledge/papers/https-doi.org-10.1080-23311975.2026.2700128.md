<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

在快速数字化的新兴国家中，提升品牌忠诚度（BL）对于推动增长和创造可持续业务至关重要。为实现这一目标，本研究通过实证检验了一个整合了数字化、伦理与运营的综合框架。研究考察了消费者生成的社交媒体传播（CGSC）、企业社会责任（CSR）和服务可及性（SA）这三个当代前因变量，如何通过品牌信任（BTR）在越南商业银行领域促进品牌忠诚度。通过在线便利抽样和滚雪球抽样收集了420名越南商业银行客户的数据，并采用偏最小二乘结构方程模型（PLS-SEM）对模型进行检验。研究结果表明，CGSC、CSR和SA均显著正向预测BTR，其中服务可及性的影响最强；而BTR又强有力地决定了BL。重要的是，分析证实了BTR在这些关系中具有完全中介作用。因此，我们的研究结果为商业从业者构建可持续客户忠诚度提供了可操作的蓝图。银行高管应通过CGSC、CSR和SA强化BTR，因为它是实现业务成功的关键桥梁。这项新颖的研究还通过揭示从数字化、伦理和运营视角经由BTR通向客户BL的完整机制，做出了重要贡献。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

In rapidly digitizing emerging countries, the enhancement of brand loyalty (BL) is essential to drive growth and create a sustainable business. To achieve this, this study empirically tests an integrated digital, ethical, and operational framework. The study examines how three contemporary antecedents, consumer-generated social media communication (CGSC), corporate social responsibility (CSR), and service accessibility (SA), foster BL through brand trust (BTR) within the Vietnamese commercial banking sector. Data collected from 420 Vietnamese commercial bank customers via online convenience and snowball sampling were analyzed using PLS-SEM to test the model. The findings reveal that CGSC, CSR, and SA significantly and positively predict BTR, with accessibility exerting the strongest influence. In turn, BTR powerfully determines BL. Significantly, the analysis substantiates the full mediating role of BTR in these relationships. Hence, our results provide an actionable blueprint for business practitioners to build sustainable customer loyalty. Banking executives should strengthen BTR through CGSC, CSR, and SA, as it serves as the essential bridge for business success. This novel research also contributes significantly by revealing the complete mechanism from digital, ethical, and operational perspectives to customer BL through BTR.
