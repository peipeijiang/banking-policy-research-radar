# 抵押贷款作为调控住宅房地产市场的工具

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文以克拉斯诺达尔边疆区为例，探讨了在宏观经济不稳定条件下，抵押贷款作为房地产市场调控工具的作用。研究特别关注2020–2025年这一时期，其特点是经济冲击、货币政策调整以及政府对抵押贷款市场的积极干预。基于统计数据，本研究分析了该地区抵押贷款发放量的动态变化、其在国家结构中的占比，以及抵押贷款市场发展与一级和二级住房市场价格趋势之间的关系。研究证实，在所考察的时期内，抵押贷款发挥了双重作用：一方面，它支撑了有效需求并刺激了建筑活动；另一方面，它也推动了价格加速上涨并导致结构性风险的积累。研究还识别出放大抵押贷款对克拉斯诺达尔边疆区房地产市场影响的区域特征。作者得出结论，有必要转向一种更为平衡的监管模式——该模式需同时考虑全国性趋势与地方供需条件。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

The article examines mortgage lending as a tool for regulating the real estate market under conditions of macroeconomic instability, using the Krasnodar Territory as a case study. Special attention is paid to the period 2020–2025, characterized by economic shocks, shifts in monetary policy, and active government intervention in the mortgage market. Based on statistical data, the study analyzes the dynamics of mortgage lending volumes in the region, its share in the national structure, as well as the relationship between mortgage market development and price trends in the primary and secondary housing markets. It is substantiated that mortgage lending during the period under review played a dual role: on the one hand, it supported effective demand and stimulated construction activity; on the other, it contributed to accelerated price growth and the accumulation of structural risks. Regional features that amplify the impact of mortgages on the real estate market of the Krasnodar Territory are identified. The authors conclude that a transition to a more balanced regulatory model is necessary – one that takes into account both national trends and local supply and demand conditions.
