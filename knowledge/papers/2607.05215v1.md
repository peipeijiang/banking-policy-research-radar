<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

我们刻画了在高维固定效应线性回归中，在漂移条件下传统方差估计量的渐近行为，其中比例固定效应维度 $ρ_n = d_{K_n}/n \to ρ\in [0,1)$ 与残差处理方差 $τ_n^2 = nQ_{K_n} \to τ^2 \in (0, \infty]$ 均为非退化的。主要发现有三点。第一，在严格外生性与条件同方差假设下，Cattaneo–Jansson–Newey 校正后的 $t$ 统计量对于任意 $τ^2 > 0$ 均是渐近精确的：$τ^2$ 中不存在 Stock–Yogo 式的阈值。第二，Eicker–White HC0 估计量存在固定因子 $(1-ρ)$ 的向下偏误，导致随饱和度增加而加剧的过度拒绝。第三，HC3 则以因子 $1/(1-ρ)$ 在相反方向上过度校正。留一法估计量（HC2）消除了一阶杠杆扭曲，并在同方差或设计平衡的异方差下是渐近精确的；在具有非均匀杠杆的一般异方差下，HC2 保留了额外的偏误，其阶数为 $ρ|μ- ω^2|$，我们对此进行了刻画。一项针对中欧与东欧（CEE）市场 Piotroski F-Score 收益率的实证应用展示了实际数据中预测的方差层级结构。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

We characterize the asymptotic behavior of conventional variance estimators in linear regression with high-dimensional fixed effects under a drift in which both the proportional fixed-effect dimension $ρ_n = d_{K_n}/n \to ρ\in [0,1)$ and the residual treatment variance $τ_n^2 = nQ_{K_n} \to τ^2 \in (0, \infty]$ are non-degenerate. Three findings emerge. First, under strict exogeneity and conditional homoskedasticity, the Cattaneo--Jansson--Newey-corrected $t$-statistic is asymptotically exact for any $τ^2 > 0$: there is no Stock--Yogo-style threshold in $τ^2$. Second, the Eicker--White HC0 estimator is biased downward by a fixed factor $(1-ρ)$, producing over-rejection that grows with saturation. Third, HC3 over-corrects in the opposite direction by a factor $1/(1-ρ)$. The leave-one-out estimator (HC2) removes the first-order leverage distortion and is asymptotically exact under homoskedasticity or design-balanced heteroskedasticity; under general heteroskedasticity with non-uniform leverage, HC2 retains an additional bias of order $ρ|μ- ω^2|$ that we characterize. An empirical application to Piotroski F-Score returns in CEE markets illustrates the predicted variance hierarchy in real data.

</details>
