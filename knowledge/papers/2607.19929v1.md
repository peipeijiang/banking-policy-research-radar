<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

我们研究了有限注意力如何影响替代弹性的结构性估计。在稀疏最大值模型中，均衡价格与支出份额在观测上等价于理性市场中的对应量。其弹性等于结构性弹性乘以注意力权重。基于协方差的估计量的供给端正交性条件在此衰减弹性下成立，但在真实弹性下不成立。在缺乏外部信息的情况下，该估计量收敛于衰减弹性。有限注意力会导致识别失败，而有限样本与弱工具变量修正无法解决这一问题。由于注意力不能超过1，该估计值为结构性弹性提供了一个下界。

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

We study how bounded attention affects the structural estimation of the elasticity of substitution. In a sparse-max model, equilibrium prices and expenditure shares are observationally equivalent to those of a rational market. Its elasticity equals the structural elasticity scaled by the attention weight. The supply-side orthogonality condition of the covariance-based estimator holds at this attenuated elasticity but fails at the truth. Absent outside information, the estimator converges to the attenuated elasticity. Bounded attention leads to an identification failure that finite-sample and weak-instrument corrections cannot resolve. Because attention cannot exceed one, the estimate provides a lower bound on the structural elasticity.
