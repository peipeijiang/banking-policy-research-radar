# Selection of Efficient Monetary Equilibria Through Aggregate Real Savings-Based Taylor Rule

[查看原文](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06599v1)

## 一句话结论

> 该论文通过引入基于总实际储蓄的泰勒规则，证明了在储蓄倾向经济中，该规则能引导经济走向有效货币均衡，而仅基于通胀目标的泰勒规则则可能导致无效均衡。

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文利用广义卡斯准则 $\sum^{\infty}_{t=1}(\Vert p_{t}\Vert\sum_{h\in G_{t}}\Vert e^{h}_{t}\Vert)^{-1}=\infty$，将Dognini（2026）关于消费贷款重叠世代经济中有效货币均衡存在性的结论进行了推广。这些结果表明，若经济具有储蓄倾向，则在包含异质性家庭的纯非平稳一般均衡模型中，货币均衡将会出现，从而为Hahn（1965）问题提供了一种解决方案。本文还证明，在具有储蓄倾向的经济中，非消失的相对总实际储蓄完全刻画了有效货币均衡。利用这一结果，本文表明，基于通胀上限与相对总实际储蓄下限的泰勒规则（Taylor rule）可用于控制价格水平，并引导经济趋向有效货币均衡。相比之下，仅基于通胀目标的泰勒规则虽能控制价格水平，但通常会导致经济趋向无效货币均衡。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This paper uses the generalized Cass criterion $\sum^{\infty}_{t=1}(\Vert p_{t}\Vert\sum_{h\in G_{t}}\Vert e^{h}_{t}\Vert)^{-1}=\infty$ to extend the results from Dognini (2026) regarding the existence of efficient monetary equilibria on consumption-loan overlapping generations economies. These results reveal that if the economy is prone to savings, then monetary equilibria will emerge in a pure non-stationary general equilibrium model with heterogeneous households, thus providing a solution to the Hahn (1965) problem. It is also proved that, in prone-to-savings economies, non-vanishing relative aggregate real savings fully characterize efficient monetary equilibria. I use this result to show that a Taylor rule based on an inflation ceiling and a relative aggregate real savings floor can be used to control the price level and lead the economy towards an efficient monetary equilibrium. In contrast, a Taylor rule based solely on an inflation target is able to control the price level but generally leads the economy towards an inefficient monetary equilibrium.

</details>
