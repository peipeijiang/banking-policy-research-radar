# The Labor Market Impacts of GLP-1s

[查看原文](https://ideas.repec.org/p/ces/ceswps/_12810.html)

## 一句话结论

> 该论文研究GLP-1治疗对劳动力市场的影响，发现其减少了长期病假，但对收入、劳动力参与和就业无显著影响。

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

我们利用丹麦的关联行政数据以及匹配堆叠双重差分设计，估算了GLP-1治疗对劳动力市场结果的因果影响。我们将在司美格鲁肽（Semaglutide）上市前两年内开始接受GLP-1治疗的患者，与四年后开始接受治疗的、在可观测特征上相似的患者进行了比较。研究发现，GLP-1治疗使长期病假减少了17.3%。我们估算，每位就业者因启动GLP-1治疗而获得的财政总收益约为其年劳动收入的1.3%–1.5%。在四年内，我们未检测到该治疗对收入、劳动力参与率或就业状况具有统计上显著或经济上有意义的影响。

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

We estimate the causal impacts of GLP-1 treatment on labor market outcomes using linked Danish administrative data and a matched stacked difference-in-differences design. We compare patients who initiate GLP-1 treatment during the first two years of Semaglutide availability to observably similar patients who initiate four years later. We find that GLP-1 treatment reduces long-term sickness leave by 17.3%. We estimate total fiscal benefits of GLP-1 initiation of approximately 1.3–1.5% of annual labor income per employed individual. We do not detect statistically significant or economically meaningful impacts on income, labor force participation, or employment over four years.

</details>
