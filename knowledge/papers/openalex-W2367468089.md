# An Analysis of Incentive Principles on High-education Administration

[查看原文](http://en.cnki.com.cn/Article_en/CJFDTOTAL-LQSZ200205023.htm)

## 一句话结论

> 该论文探讨了高等教育管理中应坚持公平、目标激励、物质与精神激励相结合以及内外激励整合的原则，并提出了满足员工需求、引导思想和创造激励环境的方法。

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文论证了高等教育管理必须坚持公平原则，将目标激励（target incentive）、物质激励（material incentive）与精神激励（spiritual incentive）相结合，并实现内部激励（internal incentive）与外部激励（external incentive）的有机统一。就激励方式而言，应满足员工的合理需求，引导其形成更优的思维方式，并营造充满激励氛围的环境。

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This article demonstrates that high-education administration must stick to the principles of fairness, combining target incentive, material incentive and spiritual incentive, and integrating internal incentive with external incentive. As far as the incentive methods are concerned, the staffs reasonable demand must be catered to, their thinking must be led to a better way and an environment full of incentives must be created.
