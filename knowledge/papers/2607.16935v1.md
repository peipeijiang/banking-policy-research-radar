<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

我们考虑在有限状态空间上的非马尔可夫标记点过程类中，受有界累积转移率不确定性约束的一类新型鲁棒效用最大化问题。效用函数在可容许控制类上最大化，而自然（Nature）则从受路径依赖上下界约束的可容许路径依赖累积转移率类中选择最坏情况的生物统计情景。我们证明了一个鞅最优性原理，以及一类非标准最坏情况倒向随机微分方程的存在唯一性新结果，这使得我们能够建立具有准备金依赖支付的人寿与健康保险合同的最坏情况与最佳情况预期准备金的存在唯一性。最后，我们找到了具有幂效用偏好的一类新型鲁棒消费-保险问题的显式解。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

We consider a novel robust utility maximisation problem under bounded cumulative transition rate uncertainty within the class of non-Markovian marked point processes on a finite state-space. Utility is maximised over the class of admissible controls, while Nature chooses a worst-case biometric scenario from the class of admissible, path-dependent cumulative transition rates restricted by path-dependent upper and lower bounds. We prove a martingale optimality principle and a novel existence and uniqueness result for a non-standard worst-case backwards stochastic differential equation, which allows us to establish existence and uniqueness of worst-case and best-case prospective reserves of life and health insurance contracts with reserve-dependent payments. Finally, we find an explicit solution of a novel robust consumption-insurance problem with power utility preferences.
