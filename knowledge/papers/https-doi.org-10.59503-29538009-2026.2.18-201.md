<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本项分析考察了2000年至2025年期间亚美尼亚公共债务与经济增长之间的关系。所考察的时段涵盖了亚美尼亚债务的整个历史区间，从占GDP的14.6%（2008年）到67.4%（2020年）。研究估计了线性和二次项的普通最小二乘法（OLS）模型，并进行了网格搜索阈值检验，以确定在所观测的债务区间内的任何水平上，增长与债务关系是否存在结构性突变。亚美尼亚经济增长的动态主要源于外部资本流入，特别是侨汇和移民，而非国内债务状况。本研究的结果拓展了关于亚美尼亚公共债务动态与可持续性的一系列研究。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This analysis examines the relationship between public debt and economic growth in Armenia over the period 2000-2025. The period under consideration covers the entire historical range of Armenia’s debt, from 14.6% of GDP (2008) to 67.4% (2020). The study estimates linear and quadratic OLS specifications and conducts a grid-search threshold test to determine whether there is a structural break in the growth-debt relationship at any level of the observed debt range. The dynamics of Armenia’s economic growth are largely caused by external capital inflows, particularly remittances and migration, rather than by the domestic debt position. The results of this study extend a series of studies on the dynamics and sustainability of Armenia’s public debt.
