# The Impact of Exchange Rate Fluctuations on the Trade of China: Evidence from a Dynamic Simulated ARDL

[查看原文](https://doi.org/10.1080/10971475.2026.2696626)

## 一句话结论

> 该论文利用动态模拟ARDL模型研究1990-2022年人民币汇率波动、货币供应、通胀和利率对中国贸易收支的影响，发现汇率波动短期和长期对贸易收支有显著正向影响，而通胀和利率有显著负向影响。

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文基于1990年至2022年的时间序列数据，考察了人民币汇率波动、货币供应量、通货膨胀及利率如何影响中国的贸易收支。尽管大多数传统宏观经济决定因素已被研究并报道，但本研究提供了新的视角，因为它采用了自回归分布滞后（ARDL）边界检验方法以及先进的动态模拟ARDL模型，以识别复杂的长期与短期截距项。通过单位根检验（即增广迪基-富勒检验）确认变量阶数为I(0)和I(1)，这解释了ARDL框架的适用性。边界检验结果表明，变量之间存在长期协整关系。实证证据显示，人民币汇率波动在短期和长期内均对贸易收支产生强烈的正向影响。而通货膨胀和利率则表现出非常显著的长期负向影响。研究结果表明，尽管人民币对总贸易变动的贡献相对较小，但其波动性是确保理想贸易地位的重要工具。本研究的结果为政策制定者提供了重要信息，以优化贸易货币政策并应对汇率变动，从而保障经济稳定平衡。通过动态模拟ARDL方法，本研究为理解中国在国际贸易环境中不断变化的角色提供了极为精确的预测工具。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This paper examines how the exchange rate volatility of the Renminbi (RMB), money supply, inflation, and interest rates affect the balance of trade in China based on time-series data throughout 1990 to 2022. Although most of the traditional macroeconomic determinants have been studied and reported, this study is a new input since it uses the ARDL bounds testing method as well as an advanced dynamic simulated ARDL model to identify the intricate long-run and short-run intercept. Order of variables are I(0) and I(1), confirmed by unit root testing, the Augmented Dickey-Fuller test, which explains the application of the ARDL framework. The results of the bounds test suggest that there is a long-term, co-integrating relationship between the variables. Empirical evidence indicates that volatility in the exchange rate in the RMB has a strong positive impact on the trade balance both in the short and long term. Inflation and interest rates, on the other hand, exhibit a very strong negative long-term influence. The findings indicate that although the RMB is comparatively a minor factor to the total trade variance, its volatility is an imperative instrument in ensuring a desirable trade position. The results of this study provide policy makers with important information to optimize the monetary policies on trade and dealing with currency changes in a way that would guarantee a stable economic balance. Through the dynamic simulated ARDL method, this study presents a very precise forecasting instrument in the quest to comprehend the changing role of China in international trade environment.
