# Investigation on Status of Spirit Incentive to TB prevention and Control Staff

[查看原文](https://en.cnki.com.cn/Article_en/CJFDTOTAL-GGWS201102001.htm)

## 一句话结论

> 该论文调查了中国结核病防控工作人员精神激励的现状，发现激励措施包括认可、培训、领导和目标激励，但效果有限。

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

目的 了解中国结核病防治工作中对结核病防治人员精神激励的现状。方法 在河南、甘肃、浙江三省各选取一个区和一个县作为调查现场，对三省结核病防治人员进行深度访谈，并采用主题框架分析法对访谈资料进行分析。结果 精神激励措施主要包括表彰激励、培训激励、领导激励、目标激励等。多数受访者认为精神激励的效果重要，但仍存在一些有待改进的局限性。结论 应认识到精神激励的显著效果，并根据不同环境和不同个体采取不同的精神激励措施。

</details>

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

Objective To Learn the status of spirit incentive to TB prevention and control staff in the TB control work in China.Methods one district and one county in each province of Henan,and Gansu Zhejiang were selected as the survey fields.In-depth interviews were conducted with the TB prevention and control staff in the three provinces.Interview data were analyzed with thematic framework analysis method.Results The spirit incentives measures mainly include recognition incentives,training incentives,leadership incentives,goal incentives and so on.Most interviewees thought the effect of spirit incentives was important,but also had some limitations to improve.Conclusion The significant effect of the spirit incentives and conduct different spirit incentive measures should be recognized according different environments and different individuals.
