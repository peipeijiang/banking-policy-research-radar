<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本研究评估了服务营销组合对孟加拉国商业银行客户忠诚度的影响，并探讨了客户满意度在此关系中的中介作用。采用结构化问卷和便利抽样法，收集了达卡市210名商业银行客户的数据。通过SmartPLS 4版软件运用结构方程模型（Structural Equation Modelling）对数据进行分析。研究结果表明，服务营销组合的七个要素——产品（product）、价格（price）、地点（place）、促销（promotion）、人员（people）、过程（process）及有形证据（physical evidence）——均与客户满意度和客户忠诚度呈显著正相关。结果还凸显了客户满意度作为关键机制的重要性，服务营销策略可通过该机制增强银行业中的客户忠诚度。本研究通过识别现有营销策略的优势与不足，并展示提升客户满意度如何有助于增强客户留存与忠诚度，为银行管理者提供了实用启示。从学术视角看，本研究丰富了银行业服务营销与客户行为领域的文献。鉴于服务营销组合在孟加拉国商业银行领域尚未得到充分探讨，本研究为如何通过有效的服务营销策略提升客户忠诚度、并借助客户满意度支持长期可持续性发展提供了宝贵见解。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This study evaluates the impact of the service marketing mix on customer loyalty in commercial banks in Bangladesh and examines the mediating role of customer satisfaction in this relationship. Survey data were collected from 210 customers of commercial banks located in Dhaka City using a structured questionnaire and a convenience sampling approach. The collected data were analyzed using Structural Equation Modelling through SmartPLS version 4. The findings reveal that all seven elements of the service marketing mix product, price, place, promotion, people, process, and physical evidence have significant and positive relationships with customer satisfaction and customer loyalty. The results also highlight the importance of customer satisfaction as a key mechanism through which service marketing strategies can strengthen customer loyalty in the banking sector. The study provides useful implications for bank managers by identifying the strengths and weaknesses of existing marketing strategies and demonstrating how improved customer satisfaction can contribute to stronger customer retention and loyalty. From an academic perspective, this research contributes to the literature on service marketing and customer behavior in the banking industry. As the role of the service marketing mix in Bangladesh’s commercial banking sector has not been adequately explored, this study offers valuable insights into how effective service marketing strategies can enhance customer loyalty and support long-term sustainability through customer satisfaction.
