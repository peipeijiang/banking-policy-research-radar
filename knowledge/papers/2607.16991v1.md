<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文针对成分型结果（即由底层分类过程生成的份额向量）开发了一种合成控制估计量。该估计量源自一个具有交互固定效应的随机效用模型（作用于相对系统效用），将成分映射为对数几率，其中标准凸包条件将反事实识别为捐赠者对数几率的凸组合。等价地，该估计量在艾奇逊度量（单纯形——即份额的非线性空间——的典型几何结构）下恢复弗雷歇重心，且对所有类别使用单一权重集。我还基于艾奇逊距离开发了一种安慰剂推断程序。将该方法应用于宾夕法尼亚州在《替代能源组合标准》实施后的发电结构，发现了一个显著且持续的成分转变：截至2022年，天然气超出其反事实水平近60个百分点，而可再生能源的相对占比则有所下降。

</details>

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This paper develops a synthetic control estimator for compositional outcomes, vectors of shares generated by an underlying categorical process. Derived from a random utility model with interactive fixed effects on relative systematic utilities, the estimator maps compositions to log-odds, where the standard convex hull condition identifies the counterfactual as a convex combination of donor log-odds. Equivalently, it recovers the Fréchet barycenter under the Aitchison metric, the canonical geometry of the simplex (the non-linear space of shares) using a single set of weights across all categories. I also developed a placebo inference procedure based on the Aitchison distance. An application to Pennsylvania's electricity generation mix following the Alternative Energy Portfolio Standard uncovers a large and persistent compositional shift: natural gas exceeds its counterfactual by nearly 60 percentage points by 2022, while renewables lose relative ground.
