# On the three levels of teacher-incentive methods in higher vocational colleges

[查看原文](https://en.cnki.com.cn/Article_en/CJFDTOTAL-ZJGZ200903011.htm)

## 一句话结论

> 该论文探讨高职院校教师激励的三个层次（基础、特殊、隐形），并认为针对不同群体使用不同方法可取得更好效果。

<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

许多高等职业院校一直在寻求有效的教师激励方法，而这些方法实际上存在不同层次。针对不同的激励群体采用不同的方法，能够取得更好的效果。激励方法可分为三个层次，即基础性激励、特殊性激励和隐性激励，将这些方法应用于不同的激励群体，必将带来令人满意的成效。

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

Many higher vocational colleges have been seeking effective methods of teacher incentives,which actually have different levels.The use of different methods for different incentive groups can achieve better effects.There are three levels of incentive methods,namely,basic,special and invisible ones,the use of which,for different incentive groups,will inevitably bring about satisfying effects.
