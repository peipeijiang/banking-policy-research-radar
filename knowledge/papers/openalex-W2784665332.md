<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

货币主义是一种经济学理论观点，主张通过控制货币供给及货币机制来实现经济政策目标，其核心在于维持物价稳定。尽管货币主义已不再盛行，但时至今日，货币政策仍是大型市场经济体中稳定政策的核心工具。货币政策是由中央银行控制货币供给的一套体系，包含两种政策：一是限制货币供给的政策，即通过提高利率应对通货膨胀；二是扩张性货币政策，即通过降低利率应对经济衰退。此类政策具有若干明确设定的主要目标，即经济流动性、货币平衡以及本币及其汇率的稳定性。根据与国内生产总值（GDP）的关系，货币政策可分为三种类型：扩张性政策、紧缩性政策与中性政策。货币政策的主要执行机构是中央银行，克罗地亚共和国的中央银行为克罗地亚国家银行（Croatian National Bank）。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

Monetaryism is a view in economic theory that relies on the control of money supply and monetary mechanisms in order to achieve the economic policy goals, and this is primarily about price stability. Although monetaryism is no longer in fashion, today monetary policy is the central tool of stabilization policy in large market economies. Monetary policy is a system that controls the money supply by the central bank. Monetary policy consists of two policies, which are policies restricting the money supply where an increase in interest rates results in inflation and an expansionary monetary policy offers where a reduction in interest rates leads recession. This type of policy has several main and clearly set objectives, namely economic liquidity, monetary balance and stability of the local currency and its exchange rate. There are several types of monetary policy which are determined to GDP – an expansive, restrictive and neutral policy. The main institution of monetary policy is the central bank, and a central bank of the Republic of Croatia is the Croatian National Bank.
