<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

在一个主要大国（美国、中国、印度、俄罗斯及欧盟/英国）深陷不可持续的债务驱动型模式的世界中，乌干达和卢旺达迎来了加速繁荣的历史性机遇。本战略政策文件分析了全球及非洲背景，并提出了八项优先政策，其核心包括：激进主权、市场自由、将中小企业视为国家财富的战略性税收、永久禁止公共债务、通过资产支持加密货币实现货币竞争、教育改革，以及将健康作为长期繁荣的基础。关键建议包括：切断超国家依赖、鼓励公私教育专业化、推进核能项目以及深化双边合作。本文借鉴成功经济体的实证经验，并避免过度监管的高负债国家的错误，提出了一套务实、分阶段的实施路线图。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

In a world where major powers (USA, China, India, Russia, and the EU/UK) are trapped in unsustainable debt-driven models, Uganda and Rwanda have a historic opportunity to accelerate prosperity. This strategic policy paper analyzes the global and African context and proposes eight priority policies centered on radical sovereignty, market freedom, strategic taxation that protects SMEs as the national treasure, a permanent ban on public debt, currency competition through asset-backed cryptocurrencies, education reform, and health as the foundation of long-term prosperity. Key recommendations include severing supranational dependencies, encouraging private-public education specialization, advancing nuclear energy programs, and deepening bilateral cooperation. Drawing on empirical lessons from successful economies and avoiding the mistakes of over-regulated high-debt nations, the paper provides a pragmatic, phased roadmap for implementation.
