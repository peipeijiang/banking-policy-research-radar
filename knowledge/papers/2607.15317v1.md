<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文提出了一种新的渐近分数阶随机占优规则，用于在足够长的投资期限内对前景进行排序。该新规则体现了相对风险厌恶具有负下界的决策者的共识。在收益率服从对数正态分布的假设下，我们建立了所提规则的等价条件，且无需对对数收益率的均值施加非负约束——这一约束通常是现有渐近随机占优规则所要求的。此外，为增强该渐近分数阶随机占优的可操作性，我们在决策者边际效用满足附加条件的基础上，提出了一种具有有界相对风险厌恶的渐近分数阶随机占优变体，称为广义渐近分数阶随机占优，并推导了其对应的等价分布特征。具有有界相对风险厌恶的（广义）渐近分数阶随机占优克服了现有渐近分数阶准则中分数阶参数对等价分布条件无影响的缺陷。实证案例进一步展示了新提出的规则在长期投资决策中用于资产选择的优势。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

In this paper, we propose a novel asymptotic fractional-order stochastic dominance rule for ranking prospects over a sufficiently long investment horizon. The new rule formulates the consensus of decision makers whose relative risk aversion has a negative lower bound. Under the assumption that returns are lognormally distributed, we establish equivalent conditions for the proposed rule without imposing the non-negativity constraint on the mean of log-return, a restriction usually required by the existing asymptotic stochastic dominance rules. Furthermore, to enhance the tractability of this asymptotic fractional-order stochastic dominance, we propose a variant of asymptotic fractional-order stochastic dominance with bounded relative risk aversion, referred to as general asymptotic fractional-order stochastic dominance, under an additional condition on decision makers' marginal utilities. We derive its corresponding equivalent distributional characterizations. The (general) asymptotic fractional-order stochastic dominance with bounded relative risk aversion overcomes the shortcomings of the existing asymptotic fractional-order criterion that the fractional-order parameter has no influence on the equivalent distributional conditions. Empirical examples further show the advantages of the newly proposed rules for asset selection in long-term investment decisions.
