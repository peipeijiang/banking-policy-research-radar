<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文研究了2010-2025年期间货币政策（特别是货币供应量M2）对越南股票市场收益率与波动风险的双重影响。采用基于学生t分布的EGARCH-X模型来处理金融时间序列的波动聚集性和厚尾分布特征。实证结果揭示了两项关键发现：第一，货币供应量的扩张对VN-Index具有显著正向影响，凸显了现金流在资产定价中的主导作用，这一发现与资产组合平衡理论（Portfolio Balance Theory）一致；第二，研究表明在样本期内货币供应量（M2）与市场波动风险降低存在关联，这与流动性扩张易引发短期不稳定的观点相悖。此外，结果还显示杠杆效应在统计上不显著，表明该时期市场对正负信息的反应相对对称。这些发现为越南国家银行（State Bank of Vietnam）管理货币政策、维护金融市场稳定提供了重要的政策启示。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

The paper examines the dual impact of monetary policy, specifically money supply (M2), on the returns and volatility risk in the Vietnamese stock market over the period 2010-2025. The EGARCH-X model with Student-t distribution is employed to manage volatility clustering and the fat-tailed distribution of financial time series. The empirical results represent two critical findings. First, the expansion of money supply has a positive and significant impact on the VN-Index, highlighting the leading role of cash flow in asset valuation; this finding is consistent with the Portfolio Balance Theory. Secondly, the paper shows evidence that money supply (M2) is associated with a reduction in market volatility risk within the sample period, contradicting the view that liquidity expansion tends to cause short-term instability. Furthermore, the results indicate that the leverage effect is not statistically significant, suggesting that the market reacts relatively symmetrically to positive and negative information during this period. These findings provide important policy implications for the State Bank of Vietnam in managing monetary policy, contributing to maintaining the stability of the financial market.
