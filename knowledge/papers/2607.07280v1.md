<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本文研究当绩效同时取决于努力与非酌情优势时，组织应如何联合设计评估规则并分配工人。代理人通过竞争网络连接的职位选择努力水平，而其有效优势则取决于自身类型及通过另一网络产生的溢出效应。规划者需同时选择分配方案及评估中的努力权重。均衡努力水平随职位的卡茨-博纳奇中心度（Katz-Bonacich centrality）上升而提高，随有效优势上升而下降。最优评估规则通常不同于真实产出。当努力在生产中更为重要时，规划者会降低努力权重并采用负匹配分配以强化激励；当优势更为重要时，规划者会提高努力权重并采用正匹配分配以利用溢出效应。本文还研究了要求分配满足两两稳定性的约束条件，该约束会导致产出损失，其大小取决于竞争强度。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This paper studies how organizations should jointly design evaluation rules and assign workers when performance depends on both effort and non-discretionary advantage. Agents choose effort in positions linked by a competition network, while their effective advantage depends on own type and spillovers through a second network. The planner chooses both the assignment and the effort weight in evaluation. Equilibrium effort rises with a position's Katz-Bonacich centrality and falls with effective advantage. The optimal evaluation rule generally differs from true output. When effort is more important in production, the planner lowers the effort weight and uses negative assortative assignment to strengthen incentives. When advantage is more important, the planner raises the effort weight and uses positive assortative assignment to exploit spillovers. We also study a constraint requiring assignments to be pairwise stable, which creates an output loss depending on the intensity of competition.

</details>
