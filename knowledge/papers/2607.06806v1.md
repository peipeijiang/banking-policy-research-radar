<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

AstroForge 旨在从小行星中开采铂族金属（PGM）。小行星上的储量似乎是无限的，且按当前市场价格计算，小行星采矿的毛利率将非常高。一个自然的问题是：当AstroForge成功证明铂族金属的经济性太空采矿时，是否会导致铂族金属市场崩溃？我们通过一个铂族金属市场的非稳态系统动力学模型来回答这一问题。研究发现，铂族金属的市场价格最终将降至远低于小行星采矿成本的水平，但这只有在全部供应转向地外之后才会发生。与此同时，巨额财富将被创造出来。而地球上的每一个人都将从低价铂族金属的新应用中受益。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

AstroForge seeks to mine platinum group metals (PGM) from asteroids. Asteroid reserves appear to be unlimited, and at current market price the gross margin of asteroid mining would be very high. It is natural to ask: when AstroForge successfully demonstrates economic space mining of PGM, will they cause the PGM market to collapse? We answer the question with a non-steady system dynamics model of the PGM market. We find that the market price for PGM will eventually drop towards the much lower cost of asteroid mining, but only after the entire supply has shifted off-world. In the meanwhile, huge fortunes will be made. And everybody on Earth will benefit from new applications of lower-price PGM.

</details>
