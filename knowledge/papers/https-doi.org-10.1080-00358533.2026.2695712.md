<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

加勒比小型开放经济体长期处于以结构性弱点为特征的宏观经济环境中，例如出口基础薄弱、进口依赖度高、外部商品世界价格的外生冲击以及频繁的气候风险。这些结构性缺陷过去曾制约东加勒比经济体实现可持续稳定增长的能力。在此困境下，由蒂莫西·N·J·安托万（Timothy N. J. Antoine）管理的东加勒比中央银行（ECCB）启动了一项现代化计划，旨在重塑该央行在东加勒比货币联盟（ECCU）中的定位。安托万自2016年上任以来，推动了一种以韧性和发展为导向的货币治理路径，这标志着与主导较大型发达经济体政策话语的诸多传统教条的决裂。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

Caribbean small open economies have long been exposed to a macroeconomic environment that is typified by structural weaknesses, such as small export bases, high levels of importation, exogenous shocks in world prices of external commodities and frequent climate risks. These structural vices have in the past curtailed the capacity of the Eastern Caribbean economies to pursue sustainable and steady growth. In this difficult environment, the Eastern Caribbean Central Bank (ECCB) under the management of Timothy N. J. Antoine has embarked on a modernisation programme to re-engineer the position of the central bank in the Eastern Caribbean Currency Union (ECCU). Antoine, since coming to office in 2016, has promoted a resilience-oriented and developmental approach to monetary governance, which represents a break with many traditional doctrines that have dominated the policy discourse in larger developed economies.
